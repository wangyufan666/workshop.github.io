PUBLICATION INFORMATION

Description of the Publicity Plan:

  • The workshop will be promoted via multiple channels. Initially, we will send the call for papers to internal mailing lists in our Universities, targeting all the graduate students at Tsinghua University, University College Dublin, University of Oslo and the University of Science and Technology of China. Additionally, we will send the CFP to mailing lists with research overlap to the foci of the workshop (e.g., SIGMOBILE-INTEREST@LISTSERV.ACM.ORG, NGN@jiscmail.ac.uk, and others). Last but not least, we will promote the workshop on social media (e.g., the SIGMOBILE Facebook group, the @ACMSIGMOBILE, and @ACMMobiSys twitter handlers, and on our networks on LinkedIn)

    • Audience: approximate the expected number of participants and submissions (range)

  • We expect around 12 submissions and an audience of 40 to 60 attendees based on our previous experience at ACM MobiSys.
